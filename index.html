About Me

I'm a tech educator and aspiring researcher interested in working at the intersection of AI innovation and practical application in healthcare. I design and teach undergraduate courses spanning machine learning, deep learning and mobile application development with Flutter through a project-based, hands-on approach. I co-founded the Ruzivo Research Lab with some of my student at African Leadership University (ALU) Rwanda to advance AI/ML research Applied in Africa.

My educational foundation includes a BSc. (Hons) Computer Science from ALU Rwanda and an M.S. in Engineering Artificial Intelligence (AI) from Carnegie Mellon University Africa (CMU-Africa). My research interests include AI for healthcare, medical image analysis, computational neuroscience, health informatics,bioinformatics, and NLP for low-resource languages.
